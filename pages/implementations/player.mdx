## __Methods__
### __.play()__
> Players starts playing the song from queue.
> 
> Returns ```Promise<void>```
___
### __.autoplay(Player)__
> Player auto-plays the song after the queue ends. 
> 
> Returns ```Promise<Player>```

### __.connect(options)__
> Player make the bot to join the voiceChannel
> 
> Returns: ```void```

Example:
```js copy
await Player.connect({guildId: "9347623649232434", voiceChannel: "3874928743294712", deaf: true, mute: false})
.catch(e => console.log("Missing permission to Join the voice channel"));
```
____
### __.stop()__
> Player stops playing current song and jumps to next one in the queue.
> 
> Returns: ```void```
____
### __.pause(Boolean)__
> Pause and resumes the player
> 
> Returns: ```Player```
___
### __.seek(Number)__
> Seek to a certain part of the song
> 
> Returns: ```void```
___
### __.setVolume(Number)__
> Change volume of the player
> 
> Returns: ```Player```

Example: 
```js copy
Player.setVolume(70);
```

### __.moveTo(newNode)__
> **Player Migration:** Moves the player to a new node (audio server instance) without disconnecting from the guild voice channel. Useful for load balancing, redundancy, or upgrading servers.
>
> - Throws if `newNode` is not provided, not connected, or already the playerâ€™s current node.
> - Safely migrates including current track, position, volume, pause state, filters, and voice data.
> - Sets `.migrating` state and emits debug info/events.
>
> Returns: ```Promise<Player>```

> __Migration Example__
> ```js copy
> await player.moveTo(newNode);
> // The player will continue seamlessly on the new node.
> ```
